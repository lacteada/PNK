About This Property

Welcome to this stunning contemporary villa nestled in the heart of Beverly Hills. This architectural masterpiece offers the perfect blend of modern luxury and California outdoor living, featuring soaring ceilings, designer finishes, and an open-concept floor plan bathed in natural light.

The chef's kitchen is equipped with premium stainless-steel appliances, a large center island, and custom cabinetry. The primary suite is a true retreat with a spa-like en-suite bath and a private terrace overlooking the resort-style pool. Four additional bedrooms — each with en-suite or shared baths — provide ample space for family and guests.

The backyard is an entertainer's dream: a heated infinity pool, outdoor kitchen, fire pit, and lush landscaping create the perfect Southern California lifestyle. A three-car garage and smart-home technology complete this extraordinary offering.
